Android USB Storage for Apps

September 23, 2012

by Keith Ratner

At some point, your Android will choke on updates, kicking back an “Insufficient Storage Available” message. If you’re not in the mood to spend the next two days performing a factory reset and reinstalling your apps, try moving some of your apps to USB storage. Pull up your Manage Apps settings screen:

Slide over to your USB STORAGE tab.

Go down your list of installed apps and select any unchecked items. This should bring you individual app settings screens where you can select your storage location.

The process will take a few moments, while your device tells you what it’s doing:

This process is reversible. Once your app is moved to USB storage, you have the option to move it back to phone:

Repeat this as necessary to clear your phone storage. You should now be able to resume your app updates.
